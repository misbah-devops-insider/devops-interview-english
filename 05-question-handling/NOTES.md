## Part 1 — 7 Common Interview Questions + Approach

| # | Question | Approach / Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | *"Tell me about yourself."* | Use **intro template**: name → exp → role → tools → Terraform concepts → closing (~60 sec) |
| 2 | *"What is your current role?"* | **Present Continuous** + **Present Indefinite**. 4 to 5 clean sentences. |
| 3 | *"Walk me through your CI/CD process."* | **Step-by-step:** First... Then... After that... Finally... |
| 4 | *"Why DevOps?"* | **Personal interest + business value + career growth.** Passion dikhao. |
| 5 | *"Tell me about a challenging project."* | Use **STAR method** (Lesson 05). End with numbers. |
| 6 | *"How do you handle production issues?"* | **Process:** Detect → Assess → Escalate → Resolve → Post-mortem |
| 7 | *"Do you have any questions for us?"* | **NEVER say "No".** Ask 2 to 3 smart questions (see Part 3 below). |

## Part 2 — "Buying Time" Phrases

Jab jawaab turant na soojhe, toh chup rehne se behtar hai — lekin **"umm aaa"** bilkul nahi. Yeh phrases use karo:

### 1. *"That's a great question, let me think for a moment..."*

### 2. *"In my experience, I have observed that..."*

### 3. *"While I haven't worked on this directly, my understanding is..."*

### 4. *"Could you please elaborate on the context?"*

### 5. *"I would approach this by first ___ , and then ___ ."*

Yeh phrases gold hain. Yaad kar lo. Interview mein turant kaam aayenge.

---

## Part 3 — Smart Questions TO ASK Interviewer

End mein interviewer poochta hai: *"Do you have any questions for us?"*

**Yeh sabse important moment hai.** "No" bolne se aap **disinterested** lagte ho. **2 to 3 smart questions** zaroor poocho.

### 1. *"What does a typical day look like in this role?"*

### 2. *"What tools is your team currently using?"*

### 3. *"What are the biggest challenges the DevOps team is facing right now?"*

### 4. *"What does success look like in the first 90 days?"*

### 5. *"How does the team approach learning and upskilling?"*

**In sab questions ka effect:** Aap ek **serious professional** lagte ho — sirf candidate nahi.

## Real Example — Pura Flow

**Interviewer:** *"Tell me about yourself."*
→ Use Lesson 00 ka template.

**Interviewer:** *"What tools do you use daily?"*
→ Use Pattern 2 (Lesson 03): *"I work with Terraform on Azure, Jenkins for CI/CD, and Prometheus for monitoring."*

**Interviewer:** *"How long have you been doing DevOps?"*
→ Use Present Perfect Continuous (Lesson 01): *"I have been working in DevOps for 2 years."*

**Interviewer:** *"Tell me about a challenging project."*
→ Use STAR method (Lesson 05).

**Interviewer:** *"This question is tricky — how would you redesign the entire pipeline?"*
→ Use buying time phrase: *"That's a great question, let me think for a moment..."* → Then answer.

**Interviewer:** *"Do you have any questions for us?"*
→ Ask 2 smart questions from Part 3.

---

## The Golden Rule

**Jawaab dene se aap candidate lagte ho.**
**Sawaal poochne se aap serious professional lagte ho.**

Pause lena galat nahi hai — *"umm aaa"* bolna galat hai. Buying time phrases use karo, sochho, phir confidence se jawaab do.

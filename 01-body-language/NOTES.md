## The 4 Pillars of Interview Presentation

Body language ke 4 main pillars hain. Inko alag-alag practice karo.

---

## 1. Face Expressions / Chehra

### Ye Karein

- Halka sa **smile** jab greet karo
- **Eye contact** — camera ya eyes pe focus
- Thoda **nod** karo jab interviewer bole
- Face **relaxed** rakho, stiff nahi
- Eyebrows thodi raise karo important point pe

### Ye Mat Karein

- Niche ya side mein dekhna
- **Blank face** (bored lagta hai)
- Over-smile ya nervous hasi
- Sochte waqt **frown** karna
- Apna video preview dekhte rehna (online interview)

---

## 2. Hand Movements / Haath

### Ye Karein

- Haath table par **visible** rakho
- **Open palm** gestures use karo
- Point banate waqt natural haath hilao
- Listing ke liye **finger count** karo (First, Second, Third)

### Ye Mat Karein

- **Haath fold** karke baithna (defensive lagta hai)
- Face, naak, baal, kaan chhuna
- Pen click karna, ring se khelna
- Statue ki tarah **haath still** rakhna
- Interviewer pe **ungli point** karna

---

## 3. Posture / Baithak

### Ye Karein

- **Seedhi posture**, shoulders back
- Slight **lean forward** (interest dikhata hai)
- **Stable** baithna, hilna nahi
- Chair pe **confidently** bait jao
- Dono pair zameen pe flat

### Ye Mat Karein

- **Slouch** karna ya jhuk ke baithna
- Chair pe **hil-dol** karna
- **Pair hilana** (nervousness ka sign)
- Bahut peeche lean karna (overconfident lagta hai)
- Pair cross karke baithna

---

## 4. Voice / Aawaz

### Ye Karein

- **Medium volume** mein bolo
- **Slow + clear** pace rakho
- Silent **pause** lo "umm" ki jagah
- Apni answer mein khud **interested** lago
- Tone **steady** rakho — warm aur clear

### Ye Mat Karein

- Bahut **tez** ya bahut **dheema** bolna
- **Flat / robotic** voice mein bolna
- "Umm", "Aaa", "Matlab" baar baar bolna
- **Mumble** karna ya whisper karna
- Ek hi tone mein bolna (boring)

---

## Dressing Sense / Kapde

### Boys / Ladke

**Do:**

- Formal shirt — **white / light blue** (tucked in)
- Dark trousers (**black / navy**)
- **Polished shoes** (no sneakers)
- Hair combed, clean shave / trim beard
- Belt matching shoes

**Avoid:**

- Bright colors, **jeans, T-shirts**
- Flashy watches / chains
- Strong perfume
- Unironed / wrinkled clothes
- Sneakers / floaters / sandals

### Girls / Ladkiyan

**Do:**

- Formal **kurti + pants**, ya shirt + trousers, ya simple suit
- **Neutral colors** (pastel, beige, navy)
- Minimal jewelry — small earrings
- Hair **neatly tied** ya set
- Closed-toe formal shoes

**Avoid:**

- **Heavy makeup** / bright lipstick
- Party wear, jeans, sleeveless
- Heavy / loud jewelry
- Strong perfume
- Open hair (if too long)

**Golden Rule:** *Aise dress karo ki interviewer aap par focus kare — kapdon par nahi.*

## Common Mistakes vs Correct Way

| Wrong | Right |
|---|---|
| Niche ya laptop screen pe dekhna | **Eye contact** — camera/eyes par focus |
| Haath fold ya pocket mein | Haath **table par visible**, open palm |
| "Umm... Aaa... Matlab..." baar baar | **Silent pause** lena |
| Slouch / jhuk ke baithna | **Seedhi posture**, slight lean forward |
| Face / baal baar baar chhuna | Haath **table par sthir** |
| Tez / dheema bolna | **Slow + clear** pace, steady tone |
| Bright shirt + jeans + sneakers | **Formal shirt + trousers + polished shoes** |

## The Golden Rule

**Interviewer aapke baare mein decision pehle 7 seconds mein le leta hai — words se nahi, body se.**

### Confidence Formula

**Smile + Eye Contact + Open Posture + Slow Speech + Formal Dressing = INTERVIEW READY**

## Practice Assignment

1. **Mirror practice:** Apna intro mirror ke saamne 5 baar bolo. Body language check karo.
2. **Phone recording:** Phone par video record karo. Khud judge karo:
   - Eye contact theek hai?
   - Haath kya kar rahe hain?
   - Posture sahi hai?
   - Voice clear hai?
3. **Friend feedback:** Ek dost ke saamne intro do. Honest feedback maango.
